# Workflows & Recipes

Goal-oriented chains of skills — navigate by *what you want to accomplish* rather than by tool name. Each recipe links the skills (and common alternatives) in the order you'd use them.

[Back to Skill Index](../index.md)

## Recipes

- [Bulk RNA-seq → pathways](bulk-rnaseq-to-pathways.md) — raw FASTQ reads to differentially expressed genes, enriched pathways, and a figure.
- [Single-cell RNA-seq](single-cell-rnaseq.md) — counts to integrated, annotated clusters with marker genes.
- [Germline variant interpretation](variant-interpretation.md) — a VCF to a ranked, ACMG-classified clinical report.
- [GWAS → polygenic risk](gwas-to-prs.md) — genotypes to association hits, fine-mapped loci, and polygenic scores.
- [Protein target → docking](target-to-docking.md) — a target sequence to a predicted structure and ranked ligand poses.
- [Paper from scratch](paper-from-scratch.md) — a research idea to a submission-ready, reviewed manuscript.
- [Systematic literature review](literature-review.md) — a question to a synthesized, cited review.

> [!tip]
> These are starting templates — fork a recipe note and adapt the chain to your data. To add a recipe, drop a new note in this folder and link it above.
